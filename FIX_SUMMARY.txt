================================================================================
TECHNICAL SUMMARY
================================================================================

The fix implements proper count recalculation by:

1. Updating the company object with new status
2. Filtering the entire company list to count each status:
   - Pending: companies with no status OR status = 'PENDING'
   - Verified: companies with status = 'VERIFIED'
   - Rejected: companies with status = 'REJECTED'
3. Calling loadStats() to sync with backend

This ensures:
- Counts are always accurate
- All status transitions are handled correctly
- Data persists across page refreshes
